About Youth Tech Link

Empowering students through technology and innovation

Youth Tech Link (YTL) is a vibrant student society at Pak-Austria Fachhochschule Institute of Applied Sciences & Technology, dedicated to bridging the gap between academic learning and industry demands. We welcome students from all disciplines, providing comprehensive digital literacy, career guidance, and hands-on technology training that prepares you for the modern workforce.

Core Objectives

Mentorship & Networking
